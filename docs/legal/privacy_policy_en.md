## TL;DR (The Three Things That Matter)

1. **We don't have your password.** You enter your e-Devlet/UYAP password
   directly into the Turkish Government's `giris.turkiye.gov.tr` site within
   the in-app browser. It never reaches our servers.
2. **Your data stays on your device.** Case and hearing data fetched from
   UYAP is stored only in an encrypted database on your phone.
3. **We have no backend.** This app does not use any cloud infrastructure
   that stores user data. We don't collect personal data.

---

## 1. Data We Process

### Data Stored Only on Your Device (Never Sent to Us)

- Your name, surname, bar registration number (you enter these optionally).
- Case numbers, court names, parties, hearing dates/times, courtroom, agenda
  fetched from UYAP.
- Hearing notes you write yourself.

All of this lives **only on your phone**, in an AES-256 encrypted database.
The encryption key is stored in OS-level secure storage (iOS Keychain /
Android Keystore).

### Limited Data That May Reach Us

- **Crash reports (optional):** With your consent, when the app crashes we
  receive device model, OS version, and an anonymized stack trace. No
  personal content is included.
- **Support emails:** When you email us, your email address and message
  reach us.
- **Store analytics:** Anonymous download/crash statistics provided by Apple
  App Store and Google Play. These do not contain your identity.

---

## 2. Why We Process Data

- To show you your hearing schedule.
- To write hearings into your phone's calendar.
- To send reminder notifications.
- To fix bugs (crash reports).
- To answer your support requests.

---

## 3. We Don't Share Your Data

We don't sell, share, or transfer data internationally. Lawful disclosure
obligations (court order, etc.) are reserved.

---

## 4. Retention

- Data on your device: until you uninstall the app or use "Delete all my
  data".
- Support emails: 2 years.
- Crash reports: 90 days.

---

## 5. Your Rights

Under Turkish KVKK Law (No. 6698), Article 11, you have rights to access,
rectification, erasure, and objection. See the
[Information Notice](kvkk_aydinlatma.md).

In-app shortcuts:

- **View my data:** Settings → My Data
- **Delete my data:** Settings → My Data → "Delete all"
- **Export my data:** Settings → My Data → "Export as XML"
