## English (Sekonder)

### App Title

**Law — TR Lawyer Calendar** (25)

### Short Description

**UYAP hearings auto-synced to your phone calendar. Privacy first.** (65)

### Full Description

```
LAW — HEARING CALENDAR FOR TURKISH LAWYERS

For attorneys (avukat) registered with the Turkish Bar.

Law fetches your case and hearing data from the UYAP Avukat Portal and
syncs it to your phone's native calendar, with smart reminders so you
never miss a court date.

▶ HOW IT WORKS

1. Open the app and tap "Sync".
2. Log in via the official Turkish e-Devlet gateway shown inside the
   app (your password goes directly to turkiye.gov.tr — never to us).
3. Law reads your hearings from UYAP, writes them to your calendar, and
   schedules reminders.

▶ FEATURES

★ All your hearings in one screen
★ Auto-sync to Google Calendar / Samsung Calendar
★ Smart reminders — 1 day before and 2 hours before
★ Auto-update if a hearing date changes in UYAP
★ Personal notes (UYAP sync never overwrites them)
★ Search and filter by case

▶ PRIVACY FIRST

• We DO NOT have your password. You enter it directly into the official
  e-Devlet site.
• NO BACKEND. All data lives in an AES-256 encrypted database on your
  device.
• NO data selling, NO third-party sharing.
• Full KVKK (Turkish GDPR) compliance.

▶ NOTE

Law is an independent helper app, not an official product of the
Turkish Ministry of Justice. The authoritative source for hearing data
remains the UYAP Avukat Portal.

Support: [support@DOMAIN]
```
